Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?
Click "Log In / Sign Up" in the top menu and fill in your username, email, and password.
2. Do I need to log in to book a vehicle?
Yes. Only registered users can make bookings. If you’re not logged in, you’ll be redirected to the login page.
3. How do I make a booking?
Log in, go to the Booking page, select vehicle type, dates, optional pickup location, and click "Confirm Booking".
4. What types of vehicles are available?
Sedans, SUVs, Motorbikes, and Vans.
